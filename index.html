Delete My Data

If you wish to delete your data associated with our app, please follow the instructions below:

1. Please email us at support@yourdomain.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

2. In your email, include your user ID (you can find this in your account settings or profile) and a clear request to delete all of your personal data.

3. Once we receive your request, we will process it within 7 working days.

Note: After your data is deleted, you will lose access to certain features that require your personal information. Please ensure that you are certain about your decision before submitting the request.

If you have any further questions about data privacy or need assistance, feel free to contact us.
